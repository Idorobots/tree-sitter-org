*** iterator interface
Document, Heading and RichText should support an iterator interface.
- Document iterates over all the headings contained within, including subheadings
- Heading iterates over the children
- Table iterates over the rows, which iterate over the colums.
- List iterates over the list items.
- RichText iterates over constituent parts.
