================
Simple citation
================
[cite:@key]

---

(document
  (zeroth_section
    (paragraph
      (citation
        (citation_reference
          (cite_key))))))

================
Citation with style
================
[cite/t:@key]

---

(document
  (zeroth_section
    (paragraph
      (citation
        (cite_style_name)
        (citation_reference
          (cite_key))))))
