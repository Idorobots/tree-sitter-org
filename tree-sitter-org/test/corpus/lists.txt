================
Multiple list items starting with inline markup
================
- *Plurals* (controversial): some style guides permit =the 1990's= -- others forbid it.
- *Omission in poetry*: =o'er=, ='twas=, =ne'er=.

---

(document
  (zeroth_section
    (plain_list
      (item
        first_line: (bold
          body: (plain_text))
        first_line: (plain_text)
        first_line: (verbatim
          body: (verbatim_content))
        first_line: (plain_text))
      (item
        first_line: (bold
          body: (plain_text))
        first_line: (plain_text)
        first_line: (verbatim
          body: (verbatim_content))
        first_line: (plain_text)
        first_line: (verbatim
          body: (verbatim_content))
        first_line: (plain_text)
        first_line: (verbatim
          body: (verbatim_content))
        first_line: (plain_text)))))
