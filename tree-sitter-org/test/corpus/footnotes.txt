================
Multiline inline footnote
================
An inline footnote with a very short definition: the slash is called a
solidus[fn:solidus:From Latin /solidus/, a gold coin whose abbreviation /s/ was
written in the numerator position of the price fraction.] in formal contexts.

---

(document
  (zeroth_section
    (paragraph
      (plain_text)
      (plain_text)
      (footnote_reference
        (fn_label)
        (plain_text)
        (italic
          (plain_text))
        (plain_text)
        (italic
          (plain_text))
        (plain_text)
        (plain_text))
      (plain_text))))
